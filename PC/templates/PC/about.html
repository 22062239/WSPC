About Us

Our Story

Founded in 204, WSPC began with a mission to provide top-notch computer maintenance and upgrade services. Our passion for technology drives us to offer comprehensive solutions that meet the unique needs of each client. We are currently a small team however that does not prevent us from striving to deliever top-notch service

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver reliable, efficient, and affordable computer services to our clients. We are dedicated to ensuring your technology operates at its best, whether through cleaning, optimization, or upgrades. We believe in the power of technology to enhance productivity and connectivity, and we are committed to helping you achieve that through our services.
